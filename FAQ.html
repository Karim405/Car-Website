Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a car online?
Visit the car’s page, select your preferred model and specifications, then click “Reserve Now” to fill out the reservation form and pay a deposit.
What is the warranty period for new cars?
All new cars come with a standard 3-year warranty or 60,000 miles, whichever comes first. Extended warranties are available at an additional cost.
Can I customize my car’s features?
Yes, you can customize features like color, interior, and add-ons during the reservation process. Contact the dealership for specific options.
How long does delivery take after ordering?
Delivery typically takes 4–8 weeks, depending on the car model and customization options. You’ll receive updates on your order status.
Is financing available for car purchases?
Yes, we offer financing plans with flexible monthly payments. Check the “Financing” section or contact our team for details.
